Player:

Once You click on the button start, that's it; we are gone for a ride. But be careful, in this city everybody drives like crazy. Your mission is too important!

How to play

You have 7 keys / buttons needed for a successful ride.

key-Left: This will move your bike slightly to the left of the road

Key-Up: This will increase the speed of your bike in the direction you are heading

key-Right: This will increase the speed of your bike in the direction you are heading

Key-Down: This will slow your bike and take you back a little bit

key "A": This will rotate or flip your bike to the left and keep you going in the same direction

key "D": This will rotate or flip your bike to the right and keep you going.

key "S": If your bike is rotated to the left or right; get it straight again pressing this key.

For the rest, make sure you avoid the obstacles as much as possible and Good Luck!
